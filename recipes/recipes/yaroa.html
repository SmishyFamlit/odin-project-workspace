Yaroa

Description

Yaroa is one of the best dishes in the world. It's a mix of fries, meat, cheese, ketchup, and mayo

Ingredients

  • Seasoned Shredded Chicken
  • Shredded Cheddar and Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the onions and garlic and set them aside
  2. Oil in the pan
  3. Cook the Chicken
